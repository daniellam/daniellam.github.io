Welcome to the services of Dungeon Hero (“Dungeon Hero”, “we”, “us”). We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. Some games or parts of our Services may use a more traditional registration or account set-up process where you may be asked to give us information like:

    • Payment Information

    If you purchase in-game virtual currency or item, our third-party payment processor will collect the billing and financial information it needs to process your charges. Dungeon Hero’s payment processors do not share your financial information, like credit card numbers, with Dungeon Hero, but they may share non-financial information with us related to your purchases, like your name, billing address, and the items purchased.

For these purchases, Dungeon Hero does not receive your financial information, but may receive non-financial information related to your purchases, like your name, approximate physical location, and the items purchased. The information Dungeon Hero receives depends on the game you’re playing and the third-party application.
